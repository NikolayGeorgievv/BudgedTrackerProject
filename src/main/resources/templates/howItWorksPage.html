How everything works?

It's actually really easy!

It's okay if things seems a bit confusing. Let us clear the fog and explain how everything works! On the left you can view various information about all the features that we provide: Accounts, Expenses and of course our Goals!

Accounts

When you register we automatically create an account for you. You can use this account or create your own. Creating your own account is very simple: on the sidebar, click the plus icon next to "My Accounts" Enter the requested information and create your account! Keep in mind you can always edit your account name, even the one we created for you. After you created your account, add funds to it, and you are ready to go! Please keep in mind that your accounts could have negative value and transactions will be occurring. To remind you that you need to add funds, we will display your current account balance in red. To add funds to an account or to change it's name click on the corresponding "Edit account" button.

Expenses

This part could be the hardest to follow, because we try to provide all the options you could need. But hey, once you get a glance at it, you will be managing your finances effortless. To create an expense, we pretty much follow the steps for creating an account: click on the plus icon next to "My Bills" and fill up the required information. This is the neat part: you can categorize your expenses, you can enter various period payments, assign different accounts to them. You can use the buttons "Weekly", "Monthly", "Yearly", "One time buy" to choose the exact payment period for your bill. For example if you pay your Electricity every 25th of the month, you can simply select: Monthly -> 25th from the dropdown and assign the amount required. If something changes, you can always edit the expenses information or delete the expense.

Goals

Here comes our favorite part! Goals and dreams should be a part of everyone's life, so we provide a feature where you can add and track your goals. To add a goal, we simply go to the sidebar and this time click on the heart button, next to "My Goals". Enter the required information and create your goal! You can easily track your goal by visiting "My Goals" page, located on the sidebar. There you will find all your goals, each of which will have a progress bar for easier tracking. You can edit or add funds to your goals via the button located across each goal. When a goal is completed, it will disappear from the "My Goals". The completed goals can be viewed from the little red heart button in the top-right corner of the "My Goals" page.

Transactions

You can track all your transactions in-app. You can both view the transaction for a specific account or all of your transactions. You can also export transaction history in CSV files. In case you want to export the transaction history of a specific account, you can do so by going at "My Accounts" page and click "my transaction history". You will be led to a page where you can view all the said account transaction history. In the top left corner there is a "download CSV file" button. In case you want to download a CSV file for all of your transactions, click on "My Transactions" located on the sidebar and follow the same steps.
